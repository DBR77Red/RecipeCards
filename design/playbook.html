01 — Direction

Design Principles

One clear feeling: like receiving a hand-written recipe from someone who really loves to cook.

🔥 Warm & Tactile

  • Cream parchment surfaces — paper memory, not clinical white
  • Deep espresso panels for contrast and drama
  • Terracotta orange as the voice of the brand
  • Grain, warmth, texture over cold flat design

⚡ Bold & Confident

  • Poppins 800/900 for headlines — loud, ownable
  • Cards as the hero element — big, photographic, editorial
  • Strong typographic hierarchy: nothing ambiguous
  • Generous padding, not cramped lists

✨ Modern Cozy

  • Rounded corners (20–40px) — friendly, not sharp
  • Warm shadows: rgba(28,10,0,…) not cold greys
  • Pill buttons with orange glow shadows
  • Micro-motion on every interaction (scale, fade, slide)

🃏 Card-First

  • Cream photo zone on top, espresso info panel on bottom
  • Thin orange hairline as the panel separator
  • Amber label for category, white Poppins for title
  • Compact stat row: serves · prep · cook
